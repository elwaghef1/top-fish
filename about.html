Who We Are

Top Fish Trade was founded in Nouadhibou with a single goal: to connect Mauritania’s rich pelagic resources with discerning buyers across Africa and the world.

We partner with local fishers and fleets, invest in modern processing and freezing facilities and nurture a skilled team to guarantee that every fish we ship meets rigorous standards. Our reputation is built on transparency and respect for both the sea and the communities that depend on it.

Our Mission

  • To supply premium pelagic fish while guaranteeing freshness and food safety from catch to delivery.
  • To innovate and continuously refine our processing, freezing and logistics capabilities for maximum efficiency and minimal environmental impact.
  • To create value for Mauritania through employment, training and equitable partnerships with local communities and suppliers.

Vision, Mission & Values

Vision

  • Become the benchmark for pelagic exports from Mauritania.
  • Establish long‑term relationships built on trust and quality.

Mission

  • Deliver the freshest pelagic species to our customers across Africa and beyond.
  • Champion sustainable fisheries and responsible trade practices.

Values

  • Integrity and transparency in every transaction.
  • Sustainability and respect for nature.
  • Commitment to our people and partners.

Development Strategy

Expand Capacity

Invest in equipment and facilities to increase our processing and freezing capacity while maintaining quality.

Diversify Products

Introduce new species and high‑value products to meet the needs of our diverse customers.

Promote Sustainability

Adopt responsible fishing practices and efficient resource use, including renewable energy projects.

Social & Environmental Responsibility

Environment

We reduce waste, manage fisheries responsibly and invest in green technologies to minimise our environmental footprint. Our policies emphasise ocean conservation and biodiversity.

Community

Through training programmes and partnerships with local organisations we create jobs, support education and improve healthcare. We believe in uplifting the communities that sustain us.
